June, 2023: Participation in Generative Modeling Summer School Denmark. I am pleased to announce my participation in the Generative Modeling Summer School held in Denmark, organized by the Danish Data Science Academy, the Pioneer Centre for AI (P1), and the Center for Basic Machine Learning.
